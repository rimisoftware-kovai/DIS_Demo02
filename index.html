Welcome to Dr. DIS

Nurturing Minds,
Shaping Futures

At Dr. Dasarathan International School, our credo is simple: Inspire. Explore. Excel. We nurture well-educated, disciplined citizens by providing rich, holistic education within beautiful, sweeping facilities — ensuring every student is equipped to tackle the challenges of tomorrow through the joy of learning today.

Our research-based curriculum emphasises a strong foundation in literacy, critical thinking, leadership, and boundless opportunities for creative expression. We believe every child carries within them an extraordinary potential — our role is to help them discover it.

📚
Research-Based Curriculum

🌱
Holistic Development
Academics balanced with arts, sports, leadership programmes, and community service for whole-child growth.
🤝
Safe & Nurturing Environment
A warm, inclusive campus where every student feels valued, respected, and empowered to thrive.
